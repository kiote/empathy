Empathy Quotient

The Empathy Quotient (EQ) is a 40-item questionnaire designed to measure empathy in adults. The test was developed at the University of Cambridge. It is suitable for use as a casual measure of temperamental empathy for the general population. Below is a list of statements. Please read each statement carefully and rate how strongly you agree or disagree with it by selecting the circle under your answer. There are no right or wrong answers, or trick questions.
